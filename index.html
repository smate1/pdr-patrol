Frequently Asked Questions

Will paintless dent repair damage my car's paint? ▼

No! PDR (Paintless Dent Repair) is specifically designed to preserve your original factory paint. We use specialized tools to massage the dent from behind the panel, returning it to its original shape without any painting or body filler.

How long does a typical repair take? ▼

Most small to medium dents can be repaired in 30 minutes to 2 hours. Larger jobs or hail damage may take longer, but we work efficiently to minimize your downtime. You can use your car during multi-day repairs.

Do you work with insurance companies? ▼

Yes! We work with all major insurance companies and can help you with your claim. PDR is often the preferred method for insurance companies because it's cost-effective and maintains your vehicle's value.

What types of dents can you fix? ▼

We can repair most dents including door dings, parking lot damage, hail damage, minor collision damage, and sports ball impacts. If the paint isn't cracked or the metal isn't stretched, PDR is likely possible.

How much money can I save compared to a body shop? ▼

PDR typically costs 30-60% less than traditional body shop repairs. For example, a door ding that might cost $400-600 at a body shop can often be repaired for $150-250 with PDR, plus you save time and maintain your original paint.
